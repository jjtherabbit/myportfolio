Guess My Number

The box in the middle will generate and contain a random number for the players to guess. To win thegame, the players should input their guesses in the textbox and click the check button to see any luck. If the guess number is too high, the message box will prompt the player that the guess number is "too high". Vise versa, if it's too low, then the message will be "too low". Once the player hits the correct number, he/she will get the "correct number" message and ends the game. To restart the game, the player needs to click the again button. Every time the player guesses the wrong number, the score number will decrease by 1 point(starts at 20 points.) until the player wins the game, the game will record the highest scores as the Highscore. Once the scores lower or equal to 5 points, the screen will send a warning signal. Once the score hits 0 points, the player loses the game, and the game is over.

Dice Games

In the project, the course instructor provided the HTML and CSS files. The purpose is to practice how to use DOM to render an interactive page. There is a simple pop-up game instruction when the first time loads the page. The screen splits into two opposite sides, and there will be two players to play on each side. Every round, the active player should roll the dice to get the points. Once the dice hits 1 point, all points in the current round will be clear and switch to another player. The only way to score is to press the hold button before it hits 1. The player who first collects 100 points wins the game.
